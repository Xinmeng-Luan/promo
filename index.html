PROMO: Physics-Informed Reduced-Order Modal Neural Operator for Long-time Non-smooth Friction-Induced Vibration in Bowed Strings

Xinmeng Luan, Gary Scavone

xinmeng.luan@mail.mcgill.ca

This page presents 16 evaluation cases under different combinations of bow force $F_B$ and bow velocity $v_B$. For each case, both the ground truth and PROMO predicted audio and visual results are provided. The audio signals are synthesized from the displacement at the observation point u(t,0.77L). The accompanying figure shows the PROMO prediction (top), the ground truth (middle), and the point-wise absolute error (bottom).
